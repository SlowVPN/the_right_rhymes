About The Right Rhymes

This is a dictionary of hip-hop language.

Word meanings (or senses) are defined & illustrated with examples from rap lyrics. These include information about the artist & release date, so the quotations are ordered chronologically, & below them are mapped the artists origins to surface any regional patterns in the usage.

Artist names, song titles, & map locations are linked. Where I've been able to find them, the lyrics also contain links to internal rhymes & named entities.
